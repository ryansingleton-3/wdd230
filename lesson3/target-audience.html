Target Audience

The target audience for this sight is both local business owners and citizens of the community. Local business owners can use this sight to work together to further local initiatives and let the public know about them. Local citizens can also see events in the community and information about local businesses.

Personas

Persona 1: A 43 year old orthodontist named Jeff that has 2 kids. Jeff is very active in the community and knows many of its citizens, as many of them are his customers. Jeff enjoys taking his kids to some of the events that the Metroplis Chamber of Commerce puts on, so he likes to view the upcoming events after work, on his phone.

Persona 2: A 63 year old chair on the board of directors for a local grocery store named Shirley. Shirley enjoys going to her grandkids' soccer games, and she primarily uses the Metropolis Chamber of Commerce site in order to get information on local sports teams she can sponsor. She typically accesses this information on her computer at home.

Scenarios

Scenario 1: A local parent in the community would like to see the football schedule for the high school to know whent he home games are, so she checks the website frequently to do so.

Scenario 2: Jeff wants to bring his kids to a local community service project that at the park and wants to get the details about when it begins.

Scenario 3: A new business owner access the website to view which other local businesses are members of the the Metropolis Chamber of Commerce to see what relationships might be advantageous to him and his business.

Scenario 4: Shirley would like to talk to a local bakery owner and see if a partnership would be beneficial, so she gets their contact information from the website.

Scenario 5: A visitor of the community would like to know where some fun things to do are, so they check the website to see what places of recreation the city has to offer.
